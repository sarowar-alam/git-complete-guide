About DevOps Corp

We started with one mission: make DevOps practices accessible to every engineering team, not just the big ones.

Our Story

DevOps Corp was founded in 2020 by a group of engineers who spent years watching teams struggle with slow deployments, broken builds, and siloed workflows. We built the tooling and training we wished we had.

Today, we work with engineering teams across Southeast Asia, helping them adopt Git workflows, CI/CD pipelines, and cloud-native practices that actually fit their context.

Our Values

  • 🎯 Practical over theoretical — real workflows, not textbook concepts
  • 🔄 Iterative improvement — small changes, shipped often
  • 📖 Open by default — we share what we learn
  • 🛡️ Security first — never an afterthought
